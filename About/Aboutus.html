Who are the Mental Friends?

MentalFriend is a NYC-based non profit organization decicated to bringing awareness to different mental conditions and ways you can support them. Our services focus on providing support to individuals who fall under the umbrella of the various mental conditions. At MentalFriend, we believe that the core values of an organization often start out as an internal guidepost for your staff, volunteers and board of directors. Everyone who realizes and supports the importance of mental health is a mental friend.
